Potato Mushrooms

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Yukon gold potatoes, olive oil, kosher salt, black pepper, mushrooms, garlic, sour cream, chives, eggplant, onion, bell pepper.

Pumpkin Soup

Cooking time - 1 hour

Ingredients: Pumpkin, olive oil, yellow onion, garlic, ground ginger, cumin, cinnamon, nutmeg, vegetable broth, coconut milk, salt, pepper.

Lentil Salad

Cooking time - 1.5 hours

Ingredients: Olive oil, onion, carrots, garlic, ground cumin, curry powder, dried thyme.

Miso Ramen Soup

Cooking time - 2.5 hours

Ingredients: Sesame oil, garlic, ginger, miso paste, soy sauce, vegetable broth, dried shiitake mushrooms, ramen noodles, green onions, soft boiled eggs, tofu.

Napoleon Cake

Cooking time - 8 hours

Ingredients: Puff pastry (often made from flour, butter, water, and sometimes vinegar or vodka), and for the custard, milk, egg yolks, sugar, cornstarch, butter, and vanilla extract.

Honey Cake

Cooking time - 6 hours

Ingredients: Flour, honey, sugar, eggs, butter, baking soda, sour cream, and a variety of spices such as cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg.

Cheesecake

Cooking time - 3 hours

Ingredients: Cream cheese, sugar, eggs, sour cream, vanilla extract, and a crust made from crushed graham crackers or digestive biscuits mixed with butter.

Apple Cake

Cooking time - 2.5 hours

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, butter, apples, baking powder, cinnamon, and sometimes nuts or raisins.

Mediterranean Chicken

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Chicken breasts or thighs, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, dried oregano, dried thyme, salt, pepper, cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, cucumber, feta cheese, fresh parsley.

Roast Salmon

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Salmon fillets, soy sauce, fresh ginger, garlic, honey, sesame oil, rice vinegar, green onions, sesame seeds.

BBQ Steak

Cooking time - 1.5 hours

Ingredients: Steak (ribeye, sirloin, or your preferred cut), olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, smoked paprika, chili powder, brown sugar, onion powder, cumin, Worcestershire sauce, apple cider vinegar.

Braised Beef Potatoes

Cooking time - 2.5 hours

Ingredients: Beef chuck roast, olive oil, salt, pepper, onion, garlic, beef broth, red wine (optional), tomato paste, carrots, potatoes, thyme, rosemary, bay leaves.

Eggplant Baked

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Eggplant, olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, onion, crushed tomatoes, fresh basil, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, breadcrumbs, dried oregano.

Ukrainian Salad Olivier

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Boiled potatoes, carrots, peas, pickles, eggs, cooked chicken or bologna, mayonnaise, mustard, salt, pepper.

Vinegret Salad

Cooking time - 3 hours

Ingredients: Beetroot, potatoes, carrots, pickles, sauerkraut, green peas, onion, sunflower oil, salt, pepper.

Okroshka Soup

Cooking time - 3 hours

Ingredients: Boiled potatoes, carrots, cucumbers, radishes, green onions, dill, boiled eggs, cooked meat (ham, sausage, or chicken), kefir or sour cream, water, mustard, salt, pepper.

Cheesy Cakes

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Cottage cheese or ricotta, eggs, sugar, flour, baking powder, vanilla extract, lemon zest, salt, butter or oil for frying.

Zucchini Pancakes

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Zucchini, eggs, flour, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic, fresh herbs (like dill or parsley), salt, pepper, olive oil for frying.

Ukrainian Crepes

Cooking time - 2.5 hours

Ingredients: Flour, milk, eggs, water, salt, sugar, butter for cooking, and various fillings like cottage cheese, mushrooms, meat, or jam.

Ukrainian Dumplings

Cooking time - 4 hours

Ingredients: Flour, eggs, water, salt for the dough; ground meat (pork, beef, or a mix), onion, garlic, salt, pepper for the filling.

Chicken Meatloaf for Dogs

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Ground chicken, cooked quinoa or brown rice, grated carrots, peas, spinach, eggs, olive oil.

Energiser for dogs

Cooking time - 2 hours

Ingredients: Lean beef chunks, potatoes, carrots, green beans, peas, beef broth, olive oil.

Ground Bite for dogs

Cooking time - 1.5 hours

Ingredients: Salmon fillet, brown rice, spinach, peas, carrots, olive oil.

Lucky dog for dogs

Cooking time - 2.5 hours

Ingredients: Ground turkey, sweet potatoes, green beans, zucchini, olive oil.
