Melania Greentree

Chef - iCOOK

Our chef has loved food for as long as she can remember. By the time she was 12, she was already spending most of her free time in the kitchen, cooking regularly for family and friends. She has a special gift—whatever you have in the fridge, she can turn it into something tasty! She just knows what ingredients go well together and which ones don’t.

At 19, she landed her very first job in a popular UK restaurant. She started out as an assistant, but in only a few months her hard work and talent got her promoted to chef. She’s not only passionate about cooking—she’s also responsible, reliable, and has a great sense of humor that makes everyone around her feel at ease.

And here’s a fun fact: while working as a chef, she’s also finishing her last year of studies for a bachelor’s degree in International Relations. Cooking has always been her way of sharing love, joy, and creativity with everyone she meets—and that passion is what makes her food truly special.
